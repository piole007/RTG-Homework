Mentorship Program

6 Jan – 30 Jun 2023 | Online

Riga TechGirls mentorship program “TechMentor” gathers professionals in the field of IT with the goal of providing support to women developing their talents, taking their first steps in the IT field or advancing their career further in this field.

Learn More

When is the deadline for applications?

You can apply until November 12th, 2022

What does program provide?

Support from a local or an international mentor, opportunity to network and form working groups with other participants of the program, environment where tp grow, advance and broaden your skills and knowledge

What is the selection criteria?

We evaluate you motivation and goals. The assessment will be carried out by a special working group together with mentors

What's the timeline

Program starts on 6th of January and ends on 30th of June, 2023

What competences can I train in TechMentor program?

We have mentors representing 12 tracks:

  • frontend development,
  • backend development,
  • UX/UI,
  • Quality Assurance,
  • engineering,
  • IT support/DevOps,
  • project management,
  • product management,
  • cyber security,
  • data analytics/data science,
  • business analytics, startups,
  • "Step up your game".

What is "Step up your game" mentorship?

it's for ladies with experience that want to “step up their game” in the IT field: boost their skillset, while knowing the basics already: likely finished our previous tracks, have done some work in the IT field or professionals that want to move to the IT field from a non-IT related position.
